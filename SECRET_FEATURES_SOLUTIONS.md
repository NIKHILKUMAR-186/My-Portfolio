# Bunny Protocol Secret Solutions

This file documents all secret achievement unlocks and how to trigger them.

## Achievement Solutions

- `first-step`
  - Open the hidden Bunny Protocol panel by clicking the floating Bunny icon in the bottom-right.

- `explorer`
  - Visit every major portfolio section. The portfolio tracks section IDs such as `about`, `timeline`, `now`, `skills`, `projects`, `learning`, `roadmap`, and `contact`.

- `night-watcher`
  - Hover the theme toggle for five seconds in the navigation area.

- `terminal-hacker`
  - Open the secret terminal using `Ctrl + Shift + K`.

- `bunny-whisperer`
  - Type `bunny` inside the secret terminal.

- `best-bunny-friend`
  - Click the Bunny icon ten times.

- `secret-scroll`
  - Open the Hints tab inside the Bunny Protocol panel.

- `reward-collector`
  - Open the Rewards tab inside the Bunny Protocol panel.

- `lore-keeper`
  - Open the Bunny Lore tab inside the Bunny Protocol panel.

- `secret-scout`
  - Open every panel tab at least once.

- `code-sleuth`
  - Type `unlock` in the secret terminal.

- `access-curiosity`
  - Visit the access denied page by trying to access `/vault` before the vault is unlocked.

- `hidden-trail`
  - Find five hidden clues across the portfolio. Look for subtle interactions and hidden elements that call `findHiddenClue`.

- `panel-architect`
  - Open every panel section and explore them through the Bunny Protocol panel.

- `curious-explorer`
  - Unlock every other achievement to complete the Bunny Protocol.

## Notes

- Progress is stored in localStorage under the key: `bunny-protocol-progress-v1`.
- The panels now close automatically when clicking outside the panel or using the close (`×`) button.
- Scrollbars are available inside the panel content area when content is longer than the visible space.
