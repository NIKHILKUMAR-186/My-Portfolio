## 🌟 Overview

This portfolio is more than a personal website.

It is a digital timeline of my growth as a student, developer, and problem solver.

The experience is designed around storytelling, allowing visitors to explore my education, projects, skills, and achievements through an interactive journey.

---

## 🎯 Purpose

I wanted to build a portfolio that:

- Goes beyond a traditional resume
- Highlights real projects and problem-solving
- Reflects my learning journey
- Creates a memorable experience for recruiters and developers
- Can evolve with my career over time
